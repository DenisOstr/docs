#### What is a Project?

A **Project** in Loglybase is the central entity that represents your product or service. It contains everything related to your changelog page and its settings.

Each project includes:

- A unique slug or custom domain
- Changelog entries
- Appearance settings

#### Creating a New Project

To create a new project:

1. Go to your **studio**
2. Click the **"Create Project"** button
3. Enter the project name and description (you can change it later)
