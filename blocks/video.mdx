The **Video** block lets you embed videos directly into your changelog posts.\
Ideal for demos, walkthroughs, or visual explanations of new features.

#### Features:

- Supports YouTube, Vimeo, and custom embeds
- Responsive and user-friendly playback
- Great for showcasing updates in action

![Video Block Dsgn Pn](/images/video_block_dsgn.png)

#### Example use cases:

- Product walkthroughs for new features
- Before/after comparisons
- Quick demos of UI improvements

Videos make your changelogs more engaging and help users see updates in motion.
