#### Appearance Settings

Loglybase lets you adjust how your changelog page looks:

- **Font family** – Choose from built-in fonts or use a custom one
- **Font size** – Adjust the scale of your text
- **Layout** – Toggle between compact or spacious view _(Coming Soon)_
- **Logo & Favicon** – Upload your brand assets

#### Light & Dark Mode

You can choose a default theme (light or dark), or let the user’s system preference decide.

Options include:

- Light only
- Dark only
- Auto (based on system)
