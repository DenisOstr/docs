**Loglybase** is a platform for creating, publishing, and customizing **changelog pages** for your products.\
It helps you communicate updates clearly, keep users informed, and maintain a professional and transparent release history.

Key features include:

- Dedicated changelog pages for each project
- Custom domains and branding support
- Flexible block-based editor (text, headings, versions, images, and more)

Loglybase is designed to keep your users engaged and your product updates organized.
