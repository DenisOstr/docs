The **Text** block is the most basic and essential content block.\
Use it to write paragraphs, describe changes, and add any supporting information to your changelog post.

#### Features:

- Supports basic formatting (bold, italic, links, etc.)
- Can be combined with other blocks for rich posts
- Great for providing context or detailed explanations

![Text Block Dsgn Pn](/images/text_block_dsgn.png)

#### Example use cases:

- Describing what was fixed or added
- Explaining why a change was made
- Adding context around a new feature

Use the Text block to keep your users informed with clear and readable updates.
