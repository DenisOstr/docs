The **Heading** block helps you organize your changelog post into clear sections.\
Use it to introduce major parts of your update and improve readability.

#### Features:

- Supports different heading levels (e.g., H2, H3)
- Makes long posts easier to scan
- Helps structure content logically

![Heading Block Dsgn Pn](/images/heading_block_dsgn.png)

#### Example use cases:

- Grouping related updates under a common title
- Creating sections like “New Features,” “Improvements,” or “Fixes”
- Highlighting important changes in your post

Use headings to bring clarity and structure to your changelog posts.
